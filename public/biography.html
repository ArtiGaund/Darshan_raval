Biography of Darshan Raval
Darshan Raval was Born on October 18,1994.
Darshan was born in Ahmedabad.His father is Rahendra Raval and mother is Rajal Raval.
Darshan Raval began his career as one of the contestants of Yo Yo Honey Singh's TV singing
reality show India's Raw Star on Star Plus in 2014.
Before India's Raw Star,he is the founder of Dip Zip band. He was the lead singer of this group.
Mera Dil Dil Dil song was sang by him when he was in this group.
He got his first Bollywood break with the song "Jab Tum Chaho" Salman Khan starrer Prem
Ratan Dhan Payo.
Raval's career took the turning point with his Bollywood entry singing the popular songs such as
It's Time to Party, Jab Tum Chaho, Bekhudi and Kheech Meri Photo.

Darshan's Father Mr. Rajendra Raval is writer. He is a Senior Journalist in Gujarat.

Darshan Raval's mother Mrs. Rajal Raval was housewife.

Darshan is an Indian Playback singer,songwriter,performer and composer from Ahmedabad,India.

His smile has a child-like innocence, and his chocolate-boy good looks and easy charm win million hearts,while soulful voice makes nubile hearts aflutter.

He had won Ahmedabad Times Most Desirable Man of 2017.
In 2016,the GIFA Award was presented to him for being the Best Playback Singer(Male).
For Darshan Raval 2018 was really memorable year as it gave hime blockbuster songs like Chogada Tara from LoveYari and Kamariya from Mitron. The Two tracks are currently on #1 and #2 respectively on Mirchi Top 20.
